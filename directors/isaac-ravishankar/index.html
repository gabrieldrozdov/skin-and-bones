ABOUT

Isaac Ravishankar

Iassc Ravishankara views the world through a lens of discovery, which makes sense for the child of a scientist and a doctor. His degree in physics speaks to his pursuit of universal truths, but also his search for new ideas, new people and new stories. A deep believer that art can be a force that drives society forward, Isaac co-founded OMG Everywhere. OMG Everywhere is a non-profit that provides free film and music workshops for kids in LA, NY, Oakland and London. How great is that?
